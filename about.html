About Me

Who I Am

I've always been drawn to things that move — cars, bikes, planes, anything built to go faster and further than what came before. That curiosity is the thread running through everything I do, pulling me toward new skills, new questions, and new versions of myself.

I believe you only live once, so I try to run like the world's on fire toward whatever I'm chasing — and if I get there, once is enough. I'm as determined to finish what I start as I am curious to begin it, always working to grow not just my skills but who I am. When I'm not building or learning, you'll find me reading, writing, or losing myself in music.
